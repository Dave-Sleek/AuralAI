LinguaEcho V1.0

Select a language and click the microphone to speak.



Tips1: Use "search for" or "what is" as command. E.g "Search for drones" or "what is Chemistry"

Tips2: To open any social media app on your phone simply use the command "open+app name" e.g say "open facebook"

Tips3: To play music use "open spotify to open spotify or apple music app" or "play song+song name, to play song on spotify" as command. E.g "Play song Winning"

Tips4: Music Players available (Spotify, Apple music, Youtube music)
